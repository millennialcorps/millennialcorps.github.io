Millennial Minded

The Millennial Corps is a collective of open minded warehouse employees. The group's design is to develop an “intrapreneur” culture for generating ideas from the bottom up while blending generations of Costco Employees. 

Feedback Loop

Think outside the box! Bring your ideas or improvements, help blend them into our culture and speak to your generation and our future members. We continue to grow our network of Corporate contacts who provide questions for group discussion in our Feedback Loop.

Mobile Friendly

Feedback is simple. Ideas, suggestions and feedback are all submitted via mobile device. Group meetings are held for round table disscussion to expand on ideas,
face to face.

Focus Areas

Millennial Corps meetings have 3 focus areas:
#Reach, #Merch &#Innovations.
Each focus is an umbrella for member service, communication, products and improvements.

Reach

Increase Audience

Next generation growth focused ideas through new marketing initiatives.

Merch

Create Demand

New products and services that are appealing to next generation and current members.

Innovations

Continuous Improvement

Innovation ideas to current systems, programs & policies that are currently in place in the Warehouse, Online & Mobile.
